## Notes for Instructors

- The PDFs are Gemini-generated curriculum documents used as source material during programme development. They are reference documents, not participant materials.
- The authoritative content for participants is the markdown modules in `modules/`.
- Module 7 (Real-World Roles) is original material not present in the PDF source documents — it was synthesised from real operational experience with the Podzone Agent Team.
- When the PDF content and the markdown modules diverge, the markdown modules take precedence. The PDFs reflect an earlier version of the curriculum; the markdown files reflect the current edition.
